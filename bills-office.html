About Us

Bill's Office is an offline and free set of applications that allow you to do whatever you need to do at school or work. We are inspired by Bill, who had so many projects to do with Discord moderating that he needed a better service to do it. However, due to bad eating habits, he died before Google Docs could connect to the internet and before Microsoft Word could be paid for. Because of this, Bill's Documents is always offline and always free. Credits page
